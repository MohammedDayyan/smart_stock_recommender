Frequently Asked Questions

How does Stockify recommend stocks?

Our AI engine synthesizes technical indicators, momentum metrics, and historical volatility to curate the top 5 stock picks mathematically tailored to your chosen timeframe and risk profile.

Is this real-time data?

Yes — stock prices are fetched live from Yahoo Finance via our backend. The dashboard reflects current market prices whenever you load or refresh.

Is it safe for beginners?

Select the "Safe" risk profile and our system prioritizes low-beta, established blue-chip companies and automatically boosts scores for low Debt/Equity stocks.

How does the AI scoring work?

Each stock is scored out of 100 using ROE, revenue growth, profit margins, momentum, analyst ratings, and sector strength. The score adapts based on the timeframe you select.

How do I buy or sell stocks?

Click Buy or Sell on any stock card, enter the quantity in the trade modal, and confirm. All trades are instantly recorded to your portfolio and transaction history. Login is required.
